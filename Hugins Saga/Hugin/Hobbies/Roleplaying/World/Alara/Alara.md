General  
Alara represent the outer reaches of Kaldashia, it consists of 5 shards that each contain three types of mana. Along the outer borders of Alara stare directly into the void_._  
The shards of Alara each border to one of the primal shards of Dominaria, and part of the elemental obelisks edge into their domain, most civilizations on Alara settle close to these Obelisks because of their immense power.
 
Living on the edge of the known world, the inhabitants of Alara are very conscious of their mortality and the fragility of the world they live in, and therefore tend to have very extreme viewpoints which they will fight and die to enforce.
 
Ferrous dragons. Inhabit these lands.
