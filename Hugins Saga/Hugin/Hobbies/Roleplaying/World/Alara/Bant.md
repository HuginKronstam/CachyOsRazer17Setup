General  
Bant is the White aligned shard of Alara, it's secondary colours are Green and Blue.
 
Nature  
Bant's beauty is unrivalled in the World of Kaldashia, open plains make up the majority of the shard complimenting them are lush forests, tropical islands, savannahs rolling hills with waterfalls and massive lakes. The shard of Bant is unique in the way that it doesn't have a capitol. Instead it has five separate region nations who all have equal say in the council of Bant. The five nations are, Akrasa, Eos and Topa known as the inner three. To the east lies the island nation of Jhess and to the west the coastal nation of Valeron. Bant is a proud shard and it has a unparalleled standing army.

Society  
Bant's social stucture is built on a rigid caste system. The inhabitants of Bant are all divided into one of 5 different castes described below.
 
The blessed: The blessed are the highest ranking people on Bant. It's members include Monarchs, religions and community leaders.  
The blessed are the people who serve at the council of Bant and they are the only ones who are able to consult the angels for advice and guidance.
 
The sighted: In this group we find the clerics, monks and seers of Bant. The religion
 
The sigiled: Prized and honoured knights
 
The mortar: Common people, loyal citizens
 
The unbeholden: The lowest of the caste, here we find the robbers, thieves whores and other scum who didn't follow the council of Bant's biding.  
Unbeholden are stuck in this caste until they have confessed and made amends, most people do this be entering army of Bant and serving their due. Once one becomes unbeholden further sinning of any kind results in immediate execution.
