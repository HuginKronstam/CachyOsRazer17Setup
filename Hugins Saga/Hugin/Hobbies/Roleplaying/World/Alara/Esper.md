General  
Esper is the Blue aligned shard of Alara it's secondary colours are White and Black.
 
Nature  
The shard of Esper is a flat windy place consisting mostly of ocean, bog, swamps, open plains, scrublands and islands. Vast underground lakes and cave systems are aplenty. The closer one is to the obelisk of _______ the more symmetric and "planned" the area looks. The winds of Esper are legendary and almost seem alive, mages say they are saturated with mana.

Society  
The primary belief system on Esper revolves around the properties of water and is known as "The Submersion Cult".  
They relish in wisdom and troubles of the mind. They despise instincts and believe in the proliferation of mana and condemn the use of passive mana. They also practice death by baptism. By drowning people they believe their life-force returns to the world.
