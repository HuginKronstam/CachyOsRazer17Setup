General  
The shard of Necra is known also as the spirit land, the culture of the clan that lives here is tied to the family and its roots. Literally.
 
Nature  
The shard of Necra is dominated by white mana and therefore the nature is primarily open plains. The capitol of the shard named Arashin is located on the edge of the taiga to the east and everything west over to the diamond shard is savannah. In the south of the shard a grand bayou is located.
 
Bestiary  
Savannah: Lions, [Vultures](http://www.aidedd.org/dnd/monstres.php?vo=giant-vulture), Rhinos, Ankheg, Raptors, Gnolls, Lamia, Dragonne, Genie, SandShark, Sandpirates, Ainok  
Bayou: Hags, Hydra, Lizardfolk, [Stirge](http://www.d20srd.org/srd/monsters/stirge.htm), Undead, Environmental effects, bug clouds, will-o-wisp  
Taiga: Wolves, Giants, Bears, Pumas, Harpy, Giant owl, Owlbear,
 
Exports

Imports  
Metals, Processed goods, Low tech machines,
 
Society  
Family values, honor, Each main branch of Capital families have a Kin-Tree which they nurture to stay in touch with their ancestors. The biggest of these tree's is in the centre of Abzan society in the capital of Arashin. A huge Baobab tree stands and between its roots the Lair of Dramoka lies. Dramoka (Shen Lung(Spirit Dragon)LN).  
Refer to clanmates as sisters and brothers.
