==Qithentis the Lustrous==  
_Young Brass Dragon_  
==Favorite topics include:==  
==•Is there such a thing as free will? Arguments include causation (determinism), environmental influences, ingrained behaviors, etc.==  
==•Multiverse creations stories. He collects them and loves to share them.==  
==•Languages. Qithentis is a polyglot and speaks seven language not including Common and Draconic. He will happily translate entire books if he has the time.==  
==•He likes learning about others academic interests, and will ask many questions.==  
==Plot Hooks:==  
==•Qithentis wants a rare book, but he is too big to fit where it is located or its socially inappropriate for a dragon to go there.==  
==•Monsters keep coming out of the underdark. Qithentis slays them on the surface, but wants the players to find their origin.==  
==•There is a puzzle, riddle, or problem that he can't solve it vexs him to no end he is looking for anyone to help.==  
==Quirks:==  
==•Qithentis has a dust mephit familiar which handles objects that are difficult for his hands to manipulate. It's name is Nix.==  
==•Qithentis frequently travels to distant lands. He wears a bag of holding on a chain around his neck to hold treasures (mostly books).==  
==Lair: He occupies an ancient abandoned temple filled with clay tablets not far from an oasis. He spends many hours put fragments back together. Qithentis allows a vast number of giant scorpions to live amongst the ruins to keep thieves away (also he likes snacking on them).==
