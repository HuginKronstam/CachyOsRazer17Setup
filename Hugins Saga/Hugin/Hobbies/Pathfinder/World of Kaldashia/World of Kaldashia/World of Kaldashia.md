**Creation**: Yaigmototh - Immortal Appretice of [Asgorath](http://forgottenrealms.wikia.com/wiki/Asgaroth)
 
Legends speak of a pressence, known as Yaigmototh . He decided to create an abudence of energy in a desolate corner of this universe. After having chosen a lonely planet for the experiment, he summoned 5 titans from across the multiverse to feed off.
 
**Cenarius:** Aspect of Life and the fruits of the earth - Livsaspektet og de frugter der kommer deraf  
**Dhümävaï:** Aspect of Death and the ambitions that lead to it - Dødsaspektet og de ambitioner som leder til det  
**Q'oq'awitz:** Aspect of Fire and the fury it brings - Ildaspektet og den passion det bringer med sig  
**Ægir:** Aspect of Water, Wisdom, patience and artificing - Vandaspekt symbol for visdom, tålmodighed og skabelse af magiske artifakter  
**Anyanwu:** Aspect of Light, flight and Honor - Aspekt, ære og evnen til at flyve
 
Their mere pressence exhumed the purest essence of magic and it merged with its neighbours creating powerfull alliances over the eons, these lands are known as the**panoramas**. Titans distant from each other in the world of Kaldashia share no such ambitions and places where their magic merged created areas of great strife, known as the **borderlands**.
 
All these lands along with the pure aspects of the titans, serve to feed **sanctuary,** the pinicle of the creators masterpiece, at the center of everything.
