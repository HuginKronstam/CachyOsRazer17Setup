**M****indset - Train your brain!**
 
Techniques

- Reframing
    - How is this the best thing that has ever happened to me?
        - Free write or list form
- Possibility
    - Write an identity statement - I am healthy because … and list reasons
- Inversion
    - Ask a question like: How can I draw more, then invert it and ask how can I draw less
        - Helps realize what you are doing wrong so I can correct these behaviors
- Perspective
    - If you were a friend who was giving you advice on this situation, what advice would you give?
- Discipline
    - Example: When you have a behavior you want to correct, make the thing you want to do easier, and the thing you want to do less, harder
- Gratitude
    - What are 3 things that you're grateful for today?
        - Something mundane
        - Something that happened by chance
        - Something that you made happen
      
    
 
**I****deate**
 
Techniques

- What is a problem you are trying to solve?
    - Write 30 things in 5 minutes
        - Don’t edit the list till the time is up
- How would ______ solve this problem?
- Open the loop
    1. Ask the question (opening the loop)
    2. Sleep
    3. Wake up and answer the question (close the loop)
        - Example: How do I make my stakeholders take interest in my projects?
          
        
 
**T****rajectory - Where do you want to go, what are your goals**
 
Techniques

- Direction
    - Set yourself a goal
        - Compile evidence for whether you are moving towards or away from the goal
            - List things that happened on the day
        - Take the list and create actionables and compile learnings.
- Day-to-day
    - Make hidden metrics visible - Money is easy to measure, but family relations etc. are harder to identify.
    - Ask the following questions
        - What excited me?
        - What drained me of energy?
        - What did I learn?
